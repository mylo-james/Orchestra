# Story 1.11: Broadcast & Policy Cascade

## Status
Spec In Progress

## Story
**As a** Scrum Master,
**I want** reliable broadcast and policy cascade mechanisms,
**so that** updates can be sent to all matching personas with dry-run and approval gates.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Dry-run with preview of target set
2. Approval gate workflow before execution
3. Full audit log with who/what/when/diff
4. Idempotent execution with retries and partial rollback
5. Deterministic ordering and failure reporting
